Subject: EarnedHome user manuals — what they are and how to use them

Hi Richard,

Attached are three EarnedHome user manuals — one for each role on the platform. Together they cover everything a person needs to use their part of the system, written in plain language with step‑by‑step instructions and a clean layout.

**What each one is, and who it's for**

1. **Broker Administrator Manual — for you.** This is the most complete of the three and it's your operating guide for the whole team. It covers signing in, reading the Leads dashboard, adding and managing loan officers, adding and managing referral partners (realtors), how leads are routed and roll up to you, updating rates, and the consent/compliance basics. If you read only one, read this.

2. **Loan Officer Manual — hand this to each loan officer you add.** It covers how they get their login, working their own leads, moving a buyer through the pipeline, adding and emailing their own realtor partners, and what buyers see. Each loan officer only sees their own leads and partners, and the guide is written from that point of view.

3. **Referral Partner Guide — hand this to each real‑estate agent you set up.** It's short and simple (agents have no login). It explains their two links — the *share link* they give to buyers, and the private *status link* they use to track their referrals — plus what they can and can't see.

**How to use them**

- Keep the Broker manual as your reference: skim it once end‑to‑end, then return to specific sections as needed.
- When you onboard a **new loan officer**, send them the Loan Officer Manual.
- When you set up a **new realtor partner**, send them the Referral Partner Guide.
- Every manual has a **Contents** page up front and a **"Common questions"** section at the back that answers the things people usually ask (sign‑in emails landing in spam, why a status shows "Connected," the difference between a loan officer and an agent, why a link stopped working, and so on) — so they shouldn't generate many follow‑up questions.

**A few notes**

- The manuals use **R Parry Financial** as the sample company throughout, so the examples read naturally. In your live system, your own company name, NMLS, and web address appear automatically.
- They reflect the current build, including the newest updates: a contact **phone on each loan officer**, each loan officer getting their **own lead alerts**, the referral partner's status page **updating on its own**, the **"Agent updates: Yes/No"** indicator, a **15‑minute auto sign‑out** for security, and turning a loan officer off **fully disabling** them and their partners.
- They describe **how the software works — not legal advice.** Please have your compliance counsel confirm the consent wording and disclosures before go‑live.
